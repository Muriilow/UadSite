What is UAD?

Universal Android Debloater is a tool written in Rust that allows users to disable unnecessary apps on their smartphones!

UAD uses Android Debug Bridge (ADB) to disable packages that users may not be able to disable by default. UAD focuses on accessibility by using a UI to make it easier to search and find the apps that you consider unnecessary.

Why UAD?

Privacy

Allows you to remove apps that hold permissions about your device.

Security

Some apps can have security problems and you won't be able to remove it without UAD.

Community

Thousands of packages documented by contributors. Here you can see what's safe to remove from your phone!

Acessibility

The simple UI makes it easier and more convinient to use than other tools.
